About Dave Graham

I’m a solo musician who sings and plays along with tracks — either guitar or keyboards depending on the song — delivering a full band experience without the full band headaches. Very few performers are in this lane, and almost no one is teaching how to do it right. That’s why I created The Pro Solo Rig Method — a way to show others how to step into the middle ground between solo acts and full bands.
