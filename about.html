Who I Am

I’m a solo musician who sings and plays along with tracks — either guitar, or keyboards depending on the song - delivering a full band experience all by myself. With years of experience, I’ve mastered the art of syncing music, vocals, and lights for shows that keep crowds engaged and coming back.

Whether it’s crowd favorites or requests, I bring a professional, unpretentious vibe to every performance.

My passion led me to develop The Pro Solo Rig Method, helping other solo artists level up their shows without the hassle of managing a band.
