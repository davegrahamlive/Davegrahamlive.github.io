About Dave Graham Live

I’m a solo musician who sings and plays along with tracks — guitar or keyboards depending on the song — delivering a full band experience without the full band overhead. Over the years, I’ve played everything from dive bars to high-end venues, weddings, and festivals.

I’m not chasing gimmicks. I focus on playing songs people know, taking requests when the moment’s right, and keeping the energy flowing from start to finish. My setup is built for efficiency and sound quality above all else.

Outside of music, I’ve been a martial arts instructor for over 20 years. The discipline, timing, and presence I learned on the mat carry directly into how I perform on stage — precise when it needs to be, loose when it’s time to jam.

That same passion for performance and streamlined gear design led me to create The Pro Solo Rig Method — a system built for the small handful of performers who even operate at this level. Want to learn more? Grab my free guide here.
